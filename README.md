# Order-dashboard-power-bi
# Order Analytics Dashboard: Sales Performance Insights

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze order and sales performance by tracking revenue, profit, customers, products, and regional trends. The dashboard enables businesses to monitor key KPIs, identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions through dynamic visualizations.
# Short Description / Purpose

The Order Analytics Dashboard is a comprehensive Power BI report that provides valuable insights into sales performance, customer behavior, and product profitability. 
It helps business managers, sales teams, and analysts monitor key metrics, compare performance across regions and categories, and improve strategic decision-making.
# Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
* 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Used for creating interactive reports and dashboards.
* 📂 **Power Query** – Data cleaning, transformation, and preparation.
* 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Developed KPIs, calculated measures, and business logic.
* 🔗 **Data Modeling** – Built relationships between Orders, Customers, Products, and Date tables for accurate reporting.
* 📁 **File Format** – `.pbix` for dashboard development and `.png` for dashboard preview.

#  Data Source
**Source:** Sample Order Sales Dataset (Excel/CSV)
The dataset includes order-level information such as customer details, order dates, product categories, sales, quantity, discounts, profit, shipping details, and regional information. The structured data model enables efficient analysis across multiple business dimensions.
#  Features / Highlights

# • Business Problem
Businesses generate thousands of sales transactions every day, making it difficult to monitor performance using spreadsheets alone.
Important business questions include:
* Which products generate the highest sales and profit?
* Which regions perform the best?
* How are sales changing over time?
* Which customer segments contribute the most revenue?
* What factors impact profitability?
# • Goal of the Dashboard
The dashboard is designed to:
* Monitor overall business performance through interactive KPIs.
* Analyze sales, profit, orders, and customer trends.
* Compare performance across product categories and regions.
* Support management with data-driven business decisions.
* Provide interactive filtering for quick business analysis.
# • Walkthrough of Key Visuals
### KPI Cards
Displays:
* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Total Quantity Sold
* Average Order Value
### Sales Trend (Line Chart)
Shows monthly or yearly sales trends, helping identify seasonal patterns and business growth.
### Sales by Category (Bar Chart)
Compares sales across different product categories to identify top-performing categories.
### Regional Performance (Map/Bar Chart)
Displays sales and profit by region, helping businesses identify strong and weak markets.
### Top Products (Bar Chart)
Ranks products based on sales or profit, highlighting best-selling items.
###  Customer Segment Analysis
Analyzes revenue contribution from different customer segments to understand buying behavior.
###  Interactive Filters
Users can filter the dashboard by:
* Region
* Category
* Customer Segment
* Order Date
* Product
# • Business Impact & Insights
* Improve sales strategy by identifying top-performing products and regions.
* Increase profitability through product and customer analysis.
* Support inventory planning using demand trends.
* Enable management to monitor KPIs in real time.
* Enhance decision-making with interactive and data-driven reports.
